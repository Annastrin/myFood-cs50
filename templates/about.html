What is it?

myProducts is an app for control your food in your fridge.

How to use it?

  1. Register
  2. Add a product in the list
  3. Reduce the quantity of the product if you want
  4. Remove the product if you want

Additional Information:

  • Products in the list are sorted by expiration date
  • You can also sort the products by name or by quantity
  • When the expiration date is coming to an end, the product's row is highlighted in red
  • When you reduce the quantity of a product to zero, the product is deleted from the list
